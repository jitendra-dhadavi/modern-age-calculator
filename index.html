🎂 Age Calculator

What is an Age Calculator?

An age calculator is an online tool that helps you calculate your exact age from your date of birth. It instantly calculates your age in years, months, and days.

How to Use the Age Calculator?

  • Select your date of birth.
  • Click the calculate button.
  • Instantly see your age in years months and days.

Benefits of Using an Online Age Calculator

Using an online age calculator helps you quickly determine your exact age without manual calculation. It is useful for official forms, school applications, and personal records.
